Access to the rest of the UI is disabled until the model is addressed. All model services are interrupted by design â€“ their purpose is to have the user focus on content on a surface that appears in front of all other surfaces.

To express that the rest of the site is inaccessible and to focus attention on the modal, surfaces behind the modal have an overlay. An overlay is a temporary treatment that can be applied to surfaces for the purpose of making content on the surface less prominent.

When scaling layouts for larger screens, adjust the modal visual presentation by setting minimum and maximum values for margins, padding and container dimensions.

Modals should maintain a minimum of 48px distance from the leading and trailing edges of the screen. On small screens, modals scale vertically until reaching 48px from the screens, top and bottom edge. On larger displays, modals can increase in size to a maximum width of 560px. When Modha reaches a maximum width, expand or contract vertically to support contact, up to a maximum height of 560px.
